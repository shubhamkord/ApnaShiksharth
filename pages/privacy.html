3. How We Collect Data

We collect your data through the following methods:

  • Automated collection: Through cookies, web beacons, and server logs as you use the Platform
  • Third-party sources: From payment gateways (transaction confirmation only) and analytics providers

4. Why We Use Your Data

We process your data under the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: To provide our educational services, process enrollments, issue certificates, and manage your account
  • Legitimate interest: To improve our Platform, analyse usage patterns, detect fraud, and ensure security
  • Consent: For marketing communications and non-essential cookies (you may withdraw consent at any time)
  • Legal obligation: To comply with applicable Indian laws and court orders

Specific uses include: personalising your learning experience, sending course progress notifications, providing customer support, processing payments, conducting research to improve content quality, and sending promotional offers (with your consent only).

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: Trusted vendors who help operate the Platform (hosting, payment processing, email delivery, analytics) — all bound by data processing agreements
  • Instructors and mentors: Course instructors may see your name and progress for courses you enroll in
  • Hiring partners: Only with your explicit consent, your profile may be shared with partner companies for placement opportunities
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, court order, or governmental authority in India
  • Business transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred to the acquiring entity, with notice to you

We never sell personal data to advertisers or data brokers. We do not use your data for behavioural advertising by third parties.

7. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Encryption at rest and in transit: All data is encrypted using AES-256 at rest; TLS 1.3 is enforced for all data transmitted to and from our servers
  • Password hashing: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with a cost factor of 12 — we never store plain-text passwords
  • JWT authentication: Session tokens are signed with strong secrets and set as httpOnly cookies to prevent XSS attacks
  • Rate limiting: Login and signup endpoints are rate-limited to prevent brute-force attacks
  • Security headers: HSTS, Content-Security-Policy, X-Frame-Options, and other protective HTTP headers are enforced
  • Regular audits: We conduct periodic security reviews and vulnerability assessments
  • Access control: Employee access to user data is restricted on a need-to-know basis with full audit logging

While we employ robust security practices, no system is 100% secure. In the event of a data breach affecting your rights, we will notify you and the appropriate authorities within the timeframes required by applicable law.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only for as long as necessary:

  • Active accounts: Data is retained for the duration of your account's existence plus 2 years
  • Deleted accounts: Data is purged within 90 days of account deletion, except where legal obligations require longer retention
  • Transaction records: Retained for 7 years as required under Indian financial laws
  • Contact form submissions: Retained for 3 years for support continuity purposes
  • Server logs: Retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes

After the applicable retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised in a manner that prevents re-identification.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for login sessions and core Platform functionality. These cannot be disabled.
  • Preference cookies: Remember your settings such as dark/light mode and language (with your consent)
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how users interact with the Platform to improve our services (with your consent)

We do not use third-party advertising cookies or sell cookie data to ad networks. You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies will affect Platform functionality.

10. Your Rights

Under India's DPDPA 2023 and applicable laws, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Correction

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your data, subject to legal retention requirements.

Right to Object

Object to processing based on legitimate interest or for direct marketing.

Right to Portability

Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.

Right to Withdraw Consent

Withdraw consent for optional processing at any time without penalty.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at support@apnashiksharth.com with subject "Data Rights Request". We will respond within 30 days. We may require identity verification before processing requests.

11. Children's Privacy

Our Platform is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent. Users aged 13–18 must have parental or guardian approval.

If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately at support@apnashiksharth.com. We will promptly delete such data from our systems.

12. Policy Changes

Your continued use of the Platform after the effective date of the revised policy constitutes your acceptance of the updated terms. If you disagree with any changes, you may delete your account before the effective date.
